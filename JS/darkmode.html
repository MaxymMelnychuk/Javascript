LIGHT MODE

What is Light Mode ?


Light mode is the opposite of dark mode. It is the default display setting for many interfaces, where the background is light (typically white or light gray) and the text is dark (usually black or dark gray). Light mode is preferred for daytime use or well-lit environments, as it offers better readability in such conditions.

What is Dark Mode?


Dark mode is a display setting for user interfaces (UIs) where the background color is dark (typically black or dark gray), and the text is light (usually white or light gray). This mode is designed to reduce the amount of light emitted by the screen, making it easier on the eyes, especially in low-light environments.

Advantages of Light Mode?

  • Better visibility in bright daylight conditions
  • Improved readability for small text
  • Familiar design, comfortable for most users
  • Versatility across various types of content and devices

Advantages of Dark Mode?

  • Redices eye strain in low-light environments
  • Save energy on OLED and AMOLED screens
  • Modern and sleek appearance, preferred by many users
  • Enhances content focus, especially at night
